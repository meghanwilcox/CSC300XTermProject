Welcome to Sip 'n Snuggles! We are a fully online, non-profit shop selling coffees, teas, and beverage accessories. We were founded with a passion for cozy drinks and animals, so we donate 100% of the profits from our shop to cat rescues across the nation. Our coffees and teas are a delicious compliment to your morning breakfest, an afternoon snack, or as a simple treat on the go. We also sell a variety of adorable mugs and beverage accesories, to provide our customers with the best experience possible! We hope you find what you are looking for at Sip 'n Snuggles, knowing that your purchase is benefitting cats in need.
